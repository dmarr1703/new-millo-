Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2024

Template privacy policy for demo purposes. Consult legal counsel for production use.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email, phone, password
  • Business Information (Sellers): Business name, address, tax ID
  • Payment Information: Credit card details (processed by Stripe)
  • Shipping Information: Delivery addresses

1.2 Usage Information

  • Pages visited and actions taken
  • Device and browser information
  • IP address and location data
  • Cookies and tracking technologies

1.3 Transaction Information

  • Purchase history
  • Product listings and sales
  • Messages between users
  • Reviews and ratings

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide Services: Process orders, manage accounts, facilitate transactions
  • Communication: Send order updates, newsletters, customer support
  • Improvement: Analyze usage, develop features, fix bugs
  • Security: Detect fraud, enforce policies, protect users
  • Marketing: Promote products, send offers (with opt-out option)
  • Compliance: Meet legal obligations, resolve disputes

3. Information Sharing

3.1 With Other Users

  • Buyers see seller business names and locations
  • Sellers see buyer names and shipping addresses for orders
  • Public profiles visible to all users

3.2 With Service Providers

  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Email Services: Transactional emails
  • Hosting Providers: Data storage
  • Analytics: Usage tracking

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes
  • Protect rights and property
  • Prevent fraud or abuse
  • Ensure platform security

4. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure password hashing
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Payment card data handled by PCI-compliant Stripe

Note: No system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request copy of your data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request account and data deletion
  • Portability: Export your data
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails
  • Object: Object to certain data processing

Contact us at [email protected] to exercise these rights.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies for:

  • Session management and authentication
  • Remembering preferences
  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • Personalized content

You can control cookies through browser settings, but some features may not work without them.

7. Data Retention

  • Account data: Until account deletion + 90 days
  • Transaction records: 7 years for tax/legal compliance
  • Analytics data: Aggregated, 2 years
  • Marketing data: Until opt-out + 30 days

8. International Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored in different countries. By using Millo, you consent to these transfers. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard contractual clauses
  • Privacy Shield compliance (where applicable)
  • EU-approved mechanisms

9. Children's Privacy

Millo is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we discover such data, we will delete it promptly.
